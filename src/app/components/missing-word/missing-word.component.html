Missing Word

Get a group of friends together for a game of Missing Word.

View Rules

  • For each round, a random two-word phrase is selected with one missing word (_____).
  • Each player privately writes down one word to fill-in-the-blank for the missing word.
  • After everyone has written an answer, players reveal their written words.
  • If exactly two players reveal the same word, they each earn 3 points.
  • If more than two players reveal the same word, they each earn 1 point.
  • Any other player earns 0 points.
  • The first player to reach 25 points win!
